About Us

Zootopia stands bold for freedom of animals. Space for animals, both quantity and quality of space, is one of the most important unresolved issues for institutions maintaining wild animals in captivity. How much room does an animal in a zoo need to thrive? How do we judge that the animal is thriving? What constitutes an adequate living space anyway? All this, precisionary queries subjugate us and incultivate the thoughts to the direction of barriers of zoo. So, here is Zootopia the beauty of virtual reality which indulges giving the users a real-life experience with exclusivity.
